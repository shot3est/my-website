Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

I recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons (cherry blossoms in March–April, autumn foliage in November). Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible — contact me via LINE for the fastest response.

What if the weather is bad?

Japan weather can be unpredictable! Light rain can actually create beautiful, moody shots. However, if conditions are unsafe or you prefer to reschedule, I offer free date changes with at least 24 hours notice.

Do you provide props, costumes, or kimono?

I can connect you with trusted kimono rental shops in Kyoto and Tokyo where you can dress up before our session. This is especially popular and adds a beautiful traditional touch to your photos. Rental costs are separate and typically ¥4,000–¥8,000.

Can I use the photos for commercial purposes?

Personal use (social media, printing, sharing with friends and family) is fully included. For commercial licensing, please mention this in your booking request and we can discuss appropriate terms.

How are the photos delivered?

All edited photos are delivered via a private, password-protected online gallery (Google Drive or WeTransfer) within 48 hours of your session. Files are full-resolution JPEGs — print-quality and Instagram-ready.
