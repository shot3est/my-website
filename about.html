こんにちは！ Hello! I'm Yuki, a professional travel photographer born and raised in Tokyo. For over eight years, I've been guiding tourists through Japan's most breathtaking landscapes and hidden corners — camera in hand, heart full of pride for this incredible country.

My mission is simple: to help you walk away from Japan not just with memories, but with photographs that genuinely tell your story. Whether it's the quiet mist of a Kyoto temple at dawn or the electric energy of Shibuya crossing at midnight — I find the light, the angle, and the moment that's uniquely yours.

I speak English and Japanese fluently, and I love working with first-time visitors and seasoned Japan travellers alike.

My Philosophy

Three Words That Guide My Work

光

Hikari — Light

Every great photo is a dance with light. I wake before sunrise and stay past golden hour to find the perfect natural glow that Japan offers in abundance.

間

Ma — Space

The Japanese concept of meaningful empty space. I believe in breathing room in composition — what's left out of a frame is as important as what's included.

和

Wa — Harmony

A relaxed subject is a beautiful subject. I create a comfortable, fun atmosphere so your genuine personality and joy shine through in every shot.
